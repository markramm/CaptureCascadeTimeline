The Capture Cascade Framework

This timeline documents a repeating pattern of institutional capture that accelerates corruption and centralizes power. Understanding this pattern helps us recognize early warning signs.

1
Crisis Creation: Real or manufactured crises generate public fear and demand for immediate action
2
Emergency Powers: Leaders claim extraordinary authority to "solve" the crisis, bypassing normal checks
3
Regulatory Capture: Industries write their own rules through favorable appointments and gutted oversight
4
Accountability Elimination: Watchdogs are fired, inspectors generals removed, whistleblowers silenced
5
System Capture: The new corrupt normal becomes entrenched, making future corruption easier

The pattern is accelerating:

1970-2000 ~8 events/year
2001-2016 ~28 events/year
2017-2020 ~68 events/year
2021-2025 ~95 events/year

Each cycle of capture makes the next cycle faster and more severe. This is not a series of isolated incidentsâ€”it's a systematic erosion of democratic safeguards.
