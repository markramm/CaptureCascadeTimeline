## Research Methodology

### For Each Company/Technology:

1. **Founding Event:**
   - Date, founders, initial funding
   - Technology description
   - Stated mission vs actual use

2. **Key Inflection Points:**
   - Major contracts (government/law enforcement)
   - Technology breakthroughs
   - Scandals/controversies
   - Regulatory actions

3. **Network Connections:**
   - Ties to Peter Thiel network
   - In-Q-Tel/CIA connections
   - Revolving door personnel
   - Cross-investments between companies

4. **Impact Events:**
   - Adoption milestones (X agencies using)
   - Privacy violations exposed
   - Lawsuits/regulatory action
   - Bans/restrictions

   - Scale of deployment
   - Government contracts
