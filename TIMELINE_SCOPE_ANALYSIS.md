## Conclusion

The timeline is **exceptionally comprehensive on Trump** and **strong on Supreme Court capture, Citizens United, Iraq War deception, and surveillance privatization**.

**Major gaps** exist for:
1. Congressional corruption (systematic undercoverage)
2. NSA/Snowden revelations (surprising omission)
